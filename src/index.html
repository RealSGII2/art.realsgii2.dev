About Me

Hi, I'm Thomas. I'm mainly a hobbyist programmer who makes Discord bots, websites, and software, but I also have other hobbies. I like to make 3D renders of cool looking things and protogens.

Sometimes I make "art" using websites. A nice example of this is https://pathos.realsgii2.dev/, where I made a pathOS terminal from SOMA.

I really enjoy Sci-Fi and spooky atmosphere.
